## Executive Summary

This report calculates your potential tax savings from equipment purchases made before December 31, 2025. Under Section 179 of the IRS tax code, businesses can deduct the **full purchase price** of qualifying equipment in the year it is placed in service.

## Qualifying Equipment

The following types of equipment qualify for Section 179 deduction:

- **Construction Equipment:** Excavators, loaders, dozers, cranes, compactors
- **Vehicles:** Trucks over 6,000 lbs GVWR, vans, specialty vehicles
- **Manufacturing Equipment:** CNC machines, presses, conveyors
- **Office Equipment:** Computers, servers, furniture
- **Software:** Business software placed in service
- **Improvements:** HVAC, roofing, fire suppression, security systems
